## 7.0.1 Known issues

These are the known issues in ThoughtSpot Release 7.0.1.

***Symptom***: A worksheet cannot be saved after it is edited.

***Conditions***: Select a worksheet, click the **Edit** button, and add one or more columns.

***Workaround***: None.
